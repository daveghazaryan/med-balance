Exercise: Mind-Body Exercises & Heart Health

Learn more about the mind-body exercises that Cleveland Clinic doctors put together to provide a powerful way for you to actively participate in your own health care, minimize pain and insomnia and promote recovery.

Mitral Valve Repair

Mitral valve repair is the surgical treatment of choice for mitral regurgitation. Learn more about it from the nation's top ranked heart center, Cleveland Clinic.

Laparoscopic Abdominoperineal Resection

Laparoscopic abdominoperineal resection removes the anus, rectum and sigmoid colon to treat cancer that has developed in those areas.

Cryoablation

Cryoablation uses extremely cold gas to freeze and destroy abnormal cells or diseased tissue. It’s often used to treat cancer and skin disorders.

Esophageal Disorders

Esophageal disorders like GERD and Barrett’s esophagus can cause painful swallowing problems. The conditions increase your risk of esophageal cancer.

Addictions: An Overview

Addiction, or the compulsion to use something to excess, is a disease with symptoms and treatments. Addictions are common and some can end in death or disability.

Calcific Tendonitis

Calcific tendonitis is characterized by a buildup of calcium deposits in your tendons. This can occur anywhere, but most people develop it in their shoulder.

Breast Reconstruction

Breast reconstruction is surgery to recreate breasts after a mastectomy. To form breasts, providers may use artificial implants or your own tissue.

Oculomotor Nerve

The oculomotor nerve (CN III) controls four of the six muscles that enable eye movement. When CN III becomes paralyzed, oculomotor dysfunction occurs.
